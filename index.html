About

Hi! I'm Joseph, a current 18 year old sophomore at Michigan State University studying Computer Science. I have been captivated by computer science since I was 11, and have created plenty of projects throughout my computer science journey.

Some of my CS skills include: Python, C++, SQLite, Javascript, Dart/Flutter, Java, and Git. Some personal hobbies I enjoy are listening to music, rock climbing, collecting watches, and hanging out with friends.

QuantumGrad

QuantumGrad is a nonprofit company that acts as a hosting site for the latest new in quantum physics, quantum computing, and other cutting edge technology. I worked as a front-end app developer using Flutter/Dart to design pages such as account and articles

Automated Twitter Account

Using Python and the twitter API, tweepy, I developed an automated twitter account that tweeted out as if it was the Spartan Statue at the Michigan State University campus. It tweets out the weather daily in East Lansing, MI, as well as when sporting events are for football, basketball, wrestling, etc.
